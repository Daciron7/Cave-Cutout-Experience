Timpanogos Cave

Timpanogos Cave National Monument was created in 1922 to protect these caves for their “unusual scientific interest”. Since then, they have been studied, photographed, and visited by thousands of people with the hope that each generation will marvel at the delicate cave formations and leave them for the next group to also study and explore. What do you hope the next generation finds when they come here?

See if you can find the crystal-clear water of Hidden Lake while on your tour. Calcite minerals can float on the surface of these still waters clustering together and creating rafts. Only the most observant can find them. Can you?
